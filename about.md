<h1>About</h1>

<p>I'm Eoin — an IT professional based between London and Ireland, with years of real-world experience managing networks, solving infrastructure problems, and watching people struggle with the gap between certification content and what actually happens on the job.</p>

<p>This site exists because I couldn't find the resource I wanted when I was studying. Something written by someone who'd actually used this stuff. Something that explained the why, not just the what. Something with labs you could actually run rather than diagrams you could only look at.</p>

<p>So I built it.</p>

<h2>What I cover</h2>

<p>Everything here is aimed at people working toward CCNA or Network+ certification, or people already working in IT who want to properly understand the fundamentals they've been winging for years. No judgment — we've all been there.</p>

<h2>The labs</h2>

<p>Every major topic comes with a downloadable Packet Tracer lab file. Open it, break it, fix it. That's how this stuff actually sticks.</p>

<h2>YouTube</h2>

<p>Most articles have a companion video on the <a href="https://youtube.com/@eoinderry" target="_blank" rel="noopener">@eoinderry YouTube channel</a>. If you learn better by watching than reading, start there.</p>

<h2>Get in touch</h2>

<p>The best place to reach me is in the comments on YouTube, or find me on Discord once the community is live.</p>
